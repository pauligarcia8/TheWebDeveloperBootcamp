Chicken

The chicken (Gallus gallus domesticus) is a domesticated subspecies of the junglefowl originally from Southeastern Asia.

Rooster or cock is a term for an adult male bird, and younger male may be called a cockerel. A male that has been castrated is a capon. An adult female bird is called a hen and a sexually immature female is called a pullet.
